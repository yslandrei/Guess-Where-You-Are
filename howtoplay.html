How to Play

Welcome to Guess where you are.

The game where you are given a random location using Google Street View
and you need to guess, based on your surroundings, where you are located

How to Play

You can navigate through the location you are given using your mouse and look for clues that might give out where you are located.

Clues you may want to keep an eye on are: Street Names, Road Signs, Arhitecture Style, Level of Infrastructure, Language, Pedestrians,
Flags, Buildings, Terrain, or Car models.

A common tactic players can use is keeping track of which metric system is being used. Observing the imperial system is almost always
a sign you are in the USA. It goes the same for Fahrenheit vs Celsius. Also, seeing cars driving on the left should always remind you
of the UK and its former colonies. And ... Japan.

Finally, if you think you've got the correct answer, submit it using the minimap on your right. You'll be prompted with a message telling
you how well you did along with the amount of points you've got. Buckle up now, you'll need to do this 4 more times in order to finish a game.
